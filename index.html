About me

Interested in microgrids, power converters, photovoltaic (PV) applications, and electronic control automated systems. Experienced in power electronics, electric machines, design of PV systems, MATLAB and PSIM simulations. I work at Government Post Graduate Jahanzeb College, Swat as a visiting lecturer. Before joining lecturership, I was pursuing MS Electronic Engineering degree at Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology, Topi, Swabi. I worked as a lab engineer and graduate assistant. Apart from teaching and research, I love writing research articles. I also love playing cricket in my free time.
