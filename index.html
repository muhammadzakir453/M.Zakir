About me

I currently hold the position of Assistant Director of Planning (Transmission & Grids) at Islamabad Electric Supply Company Headquarters in Islamabad. I am deeply passionate about scientific research, writing articles, and mentoring students. Previously, I served as a Secondary School Teacher at Govt. High School, Manai, Swat, and as a visiting lecturer at Govt. Post Graduate Jahanzeb College, Saidu Sharif, Swat. Additionally, I worked as a lab engineer and graduate assistant at Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology (GIKI), Topi, Swabi. I have earned a B.Sc. degree in Electronic Engineering from Balochistan University of IT, Engineering, and Management Sciences, Quetta, and an M.Sc. degree in Electronic Engineering from GIKI. My expertise lies in power management systems, fault detection, microgrids, power converters, photovoltaic (PV) applications, and electronic control automated systems. I possess a proficient knowledge in power electronics, electric machines, PV system design, MATLAB/SIMULINK, and PSIM simulations.
